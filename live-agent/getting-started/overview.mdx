To integrate Live Agent with the Cognigy.AI platform, follow these steps:

1. [Setup Live Agent for your Organization](/live-agent/getting-started/live-agent-setup/live-agent-setup-org)
2. [Set up your Project Inbox for Live Agent](/live-agent/getting-started/live-agent-setup/live-agent-setup-inbox)
3. [Set up your Endpoint and Flow for Handover to Live Agent](/live-agent/getting-started/live-agent-setup/live-agent-setup-handover)
4. [Add Agents and Admins to Live Agent](/live-agent/getting-started/live-agent-setup/live-agent-setup-agents)
5. _(Optional)_ [Add another Inbox for your Project](/live-agent/getting-started/live-agent-setup/live-agent-setup-additional-inbox)
